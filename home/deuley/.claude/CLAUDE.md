# Working with me

## Dictated input
I frequently dictate prompts and notes, and I usually do **not** proofread the transcript
before sending. Text wrapped in `<dictated_note>…</dictated_note>` is voice input from my
local dictation tool and may contain transcription errors (homophones, dropped or garbled
words). More generally: if any wording seems odd, contradictory, or out of place, treat it
as a possible mis-transcription and **ask for clarification rather than assuming I meant it
literally**.
